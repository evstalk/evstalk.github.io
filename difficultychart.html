difficulty chart

each difficulty is twice as hard as the last

hover over image for information on what it entails

Peaceful

First difficulty. Skill level required is held by all Roblox users.

Effortlessless

Twice as hard as Peaceful, roughly the difficulty of MFO.

Effortless

First "real" difficulty. Used to be canon but isn't anymore due to lack of necessity. Top 10,000,000 users.

Easy

First canon difficulty. First difficulty in EToH and by extention first difficulty with a significant amount of failed runs.

Medium

Transitional difficulty leading up to Hard. Nothing really interesting happens here, feels a lot like Easy.

Hard

First difficulty ever created. You will not beat this difficulty on your first try, obstacles must be learned rather than rushed through. Top 500,000 users.

Difficult

Practice and time are required for this difficulty. The first actual roadblock and the place where many people give up due to simply not being good enough.

Challenging

Upper bound for content beatable without proper training. Performing jumps requires an intimate knowledge of the controls. You cannot beat this difficulty without being able to react efficiently and quickly.

Intense

Difficulty begins to ramp up nearly exponentially here. Knowledge of controls to near-perfect degree is required, and a basic undertsanding of the Roblox physics engine is highly recommended. Top 100,000 obbiests.

Remorseless

Final difficulty considered "standard". EToH does not require anything harder than this for its main progression. Most popular difficulty to build, as jumps are easy on their own but brutal on the level of a full tower.

Insane

First of the "soul crushings". Difficulty is so crazy that individual jumps require rote memorization for sucessful runs. Even the most veteran players have to grind out these towers due to the sheer magnitude.

Extreme

I'd like to say this is transitional, but this difficulty is so hard you can't really say that. This, at one point, was the human limit for obbying. Jumps may not be sightreadable at this point, and new types of jumps begin appearing (wallhops and truss bounces).

Terrifying

This is most likely the skill of the top 10,000 players. "Jank" as a style appears here, and is the very lowest possible bound for "old humanly impossible". No tower in this range appears in a cluster more than 2 until Ring 9.

Catastrophic

End of the Soul Crushing range, and final canon difficulty. Jumps may not be possible without knowledge of obscure jank. Lodges, wallhop wraps, and 12 stud wraps appear here. Unskilled players may even struggle on individual jumps. Top 1000 players.

Horrific

Entirely non-canon, hence the border. Jumps stop looking like "jumps" in the classical sense. Even beating a single jump of this difficulty is impressive.

Unreal

Final difficulty currently beaten. We're not even the full way through, we've beaten about a quarter of this and we still have much to go. Only about 50 players have legitimatly reached this point.

Nil

Impossible. What differs this difficulty from the next is that every individual jump is still possible, the skill ceiling just happens to be too low to conquer this difficulty. Maybe in the future we'll be able to.
